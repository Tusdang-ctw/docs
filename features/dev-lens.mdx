Dev Lens is an AI-powered analytics engine that parses your Roblox Analytics CSV exports and provides actionable insights with genre-based benchmarking. Upload your data, get instant analysis — no spreadsheet skills required.

## Why Use Dev Lens?

Roblox Studio gives you raw analytics data, but interpreting it requires experience. What's a "good" D7 retention rate? Is your revenue per user high or low for your genre? Dev Lens answers these questions automatically by comparing your data against benchmarks from 11 Roblox genres.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Export from Roblox Studio">
    Open your game in Roblox Studio, go to Analytics, and export any CSV file (Retention, DAU, Playtime, Sessions, or Revenue).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload to Dev Lens">
    Drag and drop your CSV file into the Dev Lens page. You can upload up to 5 files at once for a comprehensive analysis.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get AI Analysis">
    Within seconds, you'll receive pattern detection, genre benchmarks, and specific recommendations — powered by Claude AI with rule-based fallback.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported File Types

| File Type | Metrics Analyzed | Key Benchmarks |
|-----------|-----------------|----------------|
| Retention | D1, D7, D30 retention curves | Genre-specific retention thresholds |
| DAU/DAUR | Daily active and returning users | Growth rate vs. genre average |
| Playtime | Session duration and depth | Engagement depth benchmarks |
| Sessions | Session count and frequency | Visit frequency norms |
| Revenue | Earnings, ARPPU, conversion | Monetization efficiency by genre |

## Use Cases

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Diagnosing a retention problem">
    Upload your Retention CSV. If Dev Lens flags a "D3-D7 cliff," it means players enjoy your game initially but lose interest after a few days. The AI will suggest specific interventions like progression milestones or daily rewards.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Optimizing monetization">
    Upload your Revenue CSV alongside your Sessions CSV. Dev Lens can identify if you have high engagement but low conversion (players love the game but won't pay) and suggest gamepass strategies for your genre.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Monthly health check">
    Make it a habit: export your CSVs on the 1st of each month, upload to Dev Lens, and compare against last month. Track whether your changes are moving metrics in the right direction.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
  Dev Lens is available exclusively on the **Studio** plan.
</Warning>

<Tip>
  Upload multiple file types together for the most comprehensive analysis. Retention + Revenue gives Dev Lens the context to identify monetization-retention correlations that single-file analysis misses.
</Tip>
