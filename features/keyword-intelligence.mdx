Keyword Intelligence reveals what Roblox players are actively searching for — helping you name your game, write better descriptions, and position yourself where demand exists but competition is low.

## Why Keywords Matter on Roblox

Roblox's Discover page is driven by search. When a player types "tycoon" or "horror obby", the algorithm decides which games to show. Games with titles and descriptions matching high-demand, low-competition keywords get significantly more organic traffic.

The difference between a good title and a bad one can mean **10x more impressions** from search alone.

## Use Cases

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Naming a new game">
    Before you commit to a game title, run it through the Title Scorer. Compare variations — "Zombie Tycoon" vs. "Zombie Factory" vs. "Undead Empire" — and pick the one with the best keyword performance.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Improving an existing game's discoverability">
    If your game has good retention but low traffic, the problem might be your title or description. Use Keyword Intelligence to find high-volume keywords in your genre that you're missing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Spotting emerging trends early">
    When a new keyword starts trending (e.g., "anime" or "squid game"), you'll see it in the search volume charts. Build a game around a rising keyword and ride the wave of organic search traffic.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How It Works

1. Browse the **keyword explorer** to see top keywords by search volume and competition
2. Filter by genre or theme to narrow results
3. Use the **Title Scorer** to evaluate your game title against real keyword data
4. Review suggestions and adjust your title/description for better discoverability

## Tips

<Tip>
  The best game titles combine a high-volume keyword with a unique modifier. Instead of just "Simulator", try "Pet Rescue Simulator" — it captures the "simulator" search traffic while standing out from generic titles.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Keyword data is refreshed regularly from Roblox search patterns and game discovery metrics.
</Note>
