Keyword Intelligence reveals search trends and keyword opportunities on the Roblox platform — helping you name and position your game for maximum discoverability.

## Features

- **Search volume trends** for Roblox-related keywords
- **Competition analysis** per keyword
- **Keyword suggestions** based on your game's genre and theme
- **Trending searches** showing what players are looking for right now
