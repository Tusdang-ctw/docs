The War Room is a real-time dashboard that gives you a unified view of all your tracked games — CCU, alerts, forecasts, and market context in one place.

## Features

- **Live CCU monitoring** for all tracked games
- **Alert feed** showing recent notifications
- **Quick actions** — jump to forecasts, benchmarks, or game details
- **Market context** — see how your games compare to overall market trends

<Note>
  War Room is currently in development and available as a dev-only preview.
</Note>
