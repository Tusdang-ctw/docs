The War Room is your real-time command center for monitoring every game in your portfolio. Instead of switching between individual game pages, you get a unified dashboard showing live CCU, recent alerts, forecasts, and market context — all updating in real time.

## Why Use the War Room?

If you're managing multiple Roblox games or tracking competitors, you need a single place to watch everything at once. The War Room solves the "tab sprawl" problem:

- **Before**: Open 10 browser tabs, refresh each one, mentally compare numbers
- **After**: One dashboard, all games, live updates

## Use Cases

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Studio managing a game portfolio">
    If your studio has 5+ live games, the War Room lets you monitor all of them from one screen. Set CCU alerts on each game, and the alert feed will notify you the moment any game needs attention.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Competitive monitoring during a launch">
    When launching a new game, track your top 3-5 competitors alongside your own title. Watch their CCU in real time to see if your launch is pulling players away from existing games or growing the genre overall.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Event and update rollout tracking">
    After pushing a game update or running an in-game event, open the War Room to watch the CCU response in real time. Compare against your pre-update baseline to gauge immediate impact.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How It Works

1. **Track games** from any page using the Track button (up to 50 games on Studio plan)
2. **Open the War Room** from the sidebar
3. All tracked games appear as cards with live CCU, sparkline charts, and status indicators
4. Alerts stream in as they trigger — click any alert to jump to the relevant game

## Tips

<Tip>
  Pin your most important games to the top of the War Room by tracking them first. Games appear in the order they were tracked.
</Tip>

<Note>
  War Room is currently available as a preview feature. Access it from the sidebar under dev tools.
