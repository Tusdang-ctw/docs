The Genre Heatmap provides a visual breakdown of every Roblox genre's health — showing saturation, growth, and opportunity at a glance.

## How It Works

The **Genre Opportunity Engine** analyzes multiple market signals across every Roblox genre — including player activity, growth trends, and competition levels — to produce a single opportunity score that tells you where the best openings are.

## Features

- **Heatmap view**: Color-coded grid showing opportunity scores by genre
- **Detailed breakdowns**: Click any genre for game count, average CCU, top performers, and growth trends
- **Opportunity scores**: 0–1 scale combining growth potential with competition levels

## Reading the Heatmap

The heatmap uses a quadrant system to classify genres:

- **Blue Ocean** (low saturation, high growth): The best opportunities — few competitors, growing demand
- **Red Ocean** (high saturation, low growth): Crowded genres with slowing momentum — hardest to break into
- **Emerging** (low saturation, low growth): Early-stage genres with potential but unproven demand
- **Competitive** (high saturation, high growth): Lots of competition but strong player interest — viable if your game stands out
