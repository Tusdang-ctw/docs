The Genre Heatmap provides a visual breakdown of every Roblox genre's health — showing saturation, growth, and opportunity at a glance.

## How It Works

The **Genre Opportunity Engine** scores each genre using rule-based analysis of:
- Number of active games in the genre
- Average and median CCU across genre games
- Genre growth rate (week-over-week)
- Revenue potential based on gamepass data
- Competition density (saturation)

## Features

- **Heatmap view**: Color-coded grid showing opportunity scores by genre
- **Detailed breakdowns**: Click any genre for game count, avg CCU, top performers, and growth trends
- **Opportunity scores**: 0–1 scale combining growth potential with competition levels
