Update Impact lets you log game updates and automatically measures their effect on CCU, with AI-powered analysis of what worked.

## How It Works

1. Log an update with its date, title, and categories (e.g., "New map", "Bug fixes")
2. RoLearn compares pre-update and post-update CCU averages
3. Calculates delta percentage and verdict (positive/negative/neutral)
4. AI generates a summary of the update's likely impact

## Features

- **Before/after CCU comparison** with percentage change
- **AI-powered verdict** explaining why the update helped or hurt
- **Update history** for each tracked game
- **Stats aggregation** showing which update categories perform best
