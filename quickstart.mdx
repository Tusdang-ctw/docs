## 1. Create an Account

Head to [rolearn.dev](https://rolearn.dev) and click **Sign Up**. You can register with email and password.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Visit [rolearn.dev](https://rolearn.dev) and create your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore the dashboard">
    Browse trending games, genre heatmaps, and market overview — all available on the free Explorer plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track your first game">
    Search for any Roblox game and click **Track** to add it to your watchlist. You'll get CCU history, forecasts, and alerts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up alerts">
    Configure alerts for CCU spikes, trend changes, or competitor movements. Alerts appear in your dashboard and can be sent via email (Builder+ plans).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## 3. Upgrade for More

The **Explorer** plan gives you access to trending data, basic CCU history (7 days), and limited genre insights. Upgrade to **Builder** or **Studio** for full forecasts, revenue simulation, Dev Lens AI analysis, and more.

See [Plans & Pricing](/plans) for a full comparison.
